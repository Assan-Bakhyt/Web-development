Doner

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients
Ground beef
Onion
Garlic
Yogurt
Salt + spices

Steps

  1. Mix meat, onion, garlic, yogurt, salt, spices.
  2. Shape and cook (pan/oven) until done.
  3. Serve in lavash with vegetables and sauce.

Source: recipetineats

Pasta

  • Time: 2 hour
  • Server: 3
Ingredients
Pasta (dry spaghetti/penne, etc.)
Water
Olive oil (common basic ingredient for simple pasta)
Parmesan cheese (simple classic topping)
Salt (for the cooking water)

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of water.
  2. Add salt, then add the pasta and stir.
  3. Cook until it’s “al dente” (soft but still a little firm), checking near the time on the package.
  4. Drain the pasta in a colander and mix with sauce (save a little pasta water if you want to loosen the sauce).

Source:Jamie Oliver
